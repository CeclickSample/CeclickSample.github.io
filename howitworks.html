ABOUT

Ceclick.co begins its journey with one fundamental purpose: to realize people's wish of treasuring memories. Ceclick.co is a photographer service founded by Caterina Tjuang, Erna Ang and John Kennedy who aspire to treasure your moments and be your best companion in your journey across the world.

Ceclick.co covers every aspects of photography, starting from capturing fun and beautiful moments for all events, leisure moments with family and friends, to stylish fashion photography. Time flies, memories may fade, but pictures last forever. With Ceclick.co, you will have your favorite moments delicately preserved and should you wish to walk down the memory lane, they will be ready to welcome you. Ceclick.co aims to provide you with divine snaps of your treasured moments
